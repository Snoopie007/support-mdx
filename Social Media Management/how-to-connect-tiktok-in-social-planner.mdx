Social Planner allows users to connect TikTok accounts. Right now, we provide a connection to a personal TikTok account. TikTok business account is in Roadmap.  

### Connect TikTok Personal Account

**Step 1:  First, log into the account, go to Marketing, and click on the Social Planner.**

**Step 2:  On Visiting Social Planner, there will be a wizard with a connect option, or after visiting the overview of Social Planner will have a  Setting option (Gear icon)**

**OR**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48268007117/original/voM3h2sNqN1u8Y5AQ36PPD0SQwtPTb2bNA.png?1670424355)
  
  
**Step 3:  After clicking Add account,  there will be an option to add the TikTok Personal Account (Business account is roadmap)**
  
![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48268007123/original/I_vzqd9V1Wi-4V91NCdut-mYQdjON3WufQ.jpeg?1670424355)

**Step 4: It will ask to choose the Gmail account and provide the permissions for the social planner.**

**Step 5: The next step is to  choose the TikTok account for the Social Planner**

**Step 6:  You can manage the accounts from the Setting Icon**
