### How to restore deleted contacts

**1. Click on Contacts**

![Click on Contacts](https://images.tango.us/public/edited_image_625ef275-5b7f-4de6-9fc9-d38637383543?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.4794&fp-y=0.4916&fp-z=1.0428&w=1200&ar=1920%3A907)

**2. Click on Bulk Actions**

![Click on Bulk Actions](https://images.tango.us/public/edited_image_26844722-205c-4001-bba2-32b70aa66e7f?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.3652&fp-y=0.2536&fp-z=1.9313&w=1200&ar=1920%3A907)

**3. Click on the dropdown trigger**

![Click on dropdown trigger](https://images.tango.us/public/edited_image_03cee06c-af70-497c-a77d-326c8148517e?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5038&fp-y=0.4987&fp-z=1.0075&w=1200&ar=1920%3A907)

**4. Click on Restore**

This will restore the  contact(s):
-   **Opportunities**
-   **Notes**
-   **Tasks**
-   **Appointments**
-   **Conversations**
-   **Campaigns/Workflows**

![Click on Restore](https://images.tango.us/public/edited_image_4cef99c0-8699-4d82-97a9-06bd132a6bb6?crop=focalpoint&fit=crop&fp-x=0.5595&fp-y=0.4840&fp-z=1.1319&w=1200&ar=1920%3A907)
