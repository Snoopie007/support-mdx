## Purchase Twilio Number - "Please Select Bundle/Address to Purchase Number" 

[Video Placeholder https://www.loom.com/share/69862c1cacd14f9a859b4150e21e2482?sid=0045dacb-c727-4862-843c-8b787a42f72c]

**If you are buying a phone number for countries like Australia, you might encounter this error Please select 'address' to purchase the number.**

[Image Placeholder https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204528464/original/x0Zlba-Fw1TgUHcS4jjhr9JJCBgWnXfbwA.png?1647879070]

**That's because we will need to verify** **the address and bundle in the Twilio** **SUB-account****:**

**1. Go to the  Account dropdown  on the top-right menu, click on** Subaccounts

[https://console.twilio.com/](https://console.twilio.com/?)

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204530310/original/iR9WLGt1NRbqz4hLcgF7yfeorDEluUZbfQ.png?1647879190)

**2. If there are too many subaccounts inside Twilio, you can go back to Monstro and copy the Account SIN for that location to search in Twilio:**

[Image Placeholder https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204516353/original/JwRKnlsZYcobk9Ml_p44KciuXqJ2W2d5NQ.png?1647877959]

Search based on the Twilio Subaccount SID in Monstro agency settings -> Twilio

Paste the Account SID here and click on it:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204516875/original/aibA4dz2YwTINzhWS_drP3hUwP9GG2Utjg.png?1647878035)

**3. Click into the subaccount so you will see there's an orange text on the top left:**

[Image Placeholder https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204520631/original/nk1EITrQ73KtFDT9yicb4et3hYAISQT1JA.png?1647878500]

Go to Explore Products -> Scroll down to click on  **Phone numbers**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204518019/original/EC4dWlFK4P-okMB9_GBOosuQmfUyXqNDWg.jpeg?1647878213)

Click Phone numbers:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204518383/original/VUeN7ZQE_1Pa7nGH7Xu7-h87XC6DRA-BYg.png?1647878269)

Click on  **Regulatory Compliance**  and add  **Bundles and Addresses**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204518520/original/5Puvd10iLfHKdSAsVQHspAVj_qmXd9eUVA.png?1647878296)

For Bundles, you can learn more about  [Twilio international phone number availability and their capabilities here](https://support.twilio.com/hc/en-us/articles/223183068-Twilio-international-phone-number-availability-and-their-capabilities).

For Dual Functionality (Voice and SMS enabled) Phone Numbers, please choose Mobile for the type of Phone numbers here:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204533305/original/emuLvhUjfQ7c3x_b-taE9qgHzL_04rX1zQ.png?1647879453)

Once the bundle is added, add an address as well:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/48204534829/original/pOX1k90nPh001-3co7nT7HgQQDsOdeQ90w.png?1647879721)

Once Twilio approves the bundles, the bundles and addresses will show up in the dropdown once you are trying to buy numbers in the location.
