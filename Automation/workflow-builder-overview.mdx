## Workflow Builder - Overview 

### Infinite Canvas layout

Use the "Fit to Screen" and "Zoom In/Zoom Out" buttons in the bottom left of the screen to control the view area. The Minimap in the bottom right shows the current position.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010654896/original/hiVPRYcJRMstKcpxYIlqG9Ao4a52rO8-6g.png?1697799711)  

### Add New Trigger

Click on "Add New Trigger" button to open the Workflow Triggers list

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010654999/original/wsmdd3Y6I3kzpPUf1oYlhsez4Rn-6oSHoA.png?1697799763)

**Select a Trigger from the list and configure**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010708451/original/GJgOSB7vjcVaW3x5baHqI5qsdfdRkKS9bw.png?1697835068)

### Add New Action

Click on "+" button to open the Workflow Actions list

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010654928/original/yPgQLoc0OyZUl8tOMEkVFidsO4APDlGO6w.png?1697799730)

**Select an Action from the list and configure**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010708435/original/xKkLJv37gP0mEylNnhkbRdsZF3O13q1j2g.png?1697834950)

### Stats View

Turn on the "Stats View" in the top left to view Email and SMS stats in the builder

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010655037/original/n6bLGGrcm2k1mgLF-aE3UOU77Oal9BHEwA.png?1697799786)

**Open detailed statistics model by clicking on any of the stats data**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010708069/original/EoxFI91wg_eqOAkVpqUsmB4cHnRHucdzyA.png?1697834506)

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010708138/original/bKnnXcV4rFDjnN2Hj_0mZhW0aFzZg24Yyg.png?1697834524)

### Test Workflow

Click on the "Test Workflow" button in the top right

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010655044/original/t535Z3TPKOCnJME8h4S3VQzdVA770ALh6A.png?1697799804)

**Select a contact and click on "Run Test" to start testing the workflow**

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010707982/original/LRMWk-ZCZdtLLaIwqbwZ0uaPf_Ls-F_AFQ.png?1697834308)

### Save Workflow

Click the "Save" button in the top right which indicates unsaved changes.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010655129/original/AYe7vfsDlKVuIuHbp_juObcTmkUClOdWiw.png?1697799848)

View history of changes made by clicking on the history icon in the top right

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/cdn.freshdesk.com/data/helpdesk/attachments/production/155010655239/original/v0QnN8sx5sgXj2L6iGgmCIOocF--oD_YpA.png?1697799879)

### Draft/Publish

Publish workflow or switch back to Draft by switching the toggle on the top right
